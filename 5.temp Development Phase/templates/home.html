Opti Crop Agricultural Optimisation

The main purpose of the OptiCrop aims to develop an advanced software system that utilizes data-driven insights to optimize agricultural production for different crops. By integrating key environmental factors such as Nitrogen (N), Phosphorous (P), Potassium (K) levels, soil temperature, humidity, pH, and rainfall, OptiCrop provides intelligent recommendations to farmers.
